📖 Recipes

🥖 Bruschetta Recipe

    Chop fresh tomatoes and basil.
    Mix with olive oil, salt, and pepper.
    Toast bread slices.
    Top with the tomato mixture and serve.

🥩 Filet Mignon Recipe

    Season filet with salt and pepper.
    Sear in a hot pan with butter for 3-4 mins per side.
    Finish in the oven at 375°F (190°C) for 10 mins.
    Serve with mashed potatoes and red wine sauce.

🍫 Chocolate Lava Cake Recipe

    Melt chocolate and butter together.
    Mix with sugar, eggs, and flour.
    Bake at 400°F (200°C) for 12 mins.
    Serve warm with ice cream.
